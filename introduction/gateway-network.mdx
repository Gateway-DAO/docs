## Introduction 

**Gateway Protocol is the leading way for users to share their data with people and applications across the internet.** With focus on user soverignty and privacy, Gateway is the first system to allow regulatory compliant data sharing and usage. To read more about Gateway's vision, click [here](https://google.com). 

Gateway offers seamless products for interacting with the protocol. For users, Gateway has custodial and non-custodial solutions for taking ownership of your data. 

## Network Participants 
The network has three main roles. Depending on what you are looking to accomplish, you may fit one or all three roles. Click on one to learn more:

 <CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Data Contributors" icon="gift">
   They are issuing user data into the network. 
  </Card>
  <Card title="Owners" icon="crown">
   They are the controllers of the data.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Data Consumers" icon="code-pull-request">
    They are asking and using the user data.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>Organizations are usually Contributors and/or Consumers. Normal users would usually be owners!</Tip>
