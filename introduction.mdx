**Gateway Protocol is the leading way for users to share their data with people and applications across the internet.** With focus on user soverignty and privacy, Gateway is the first system to allow regulatory compliant data sharing and usage.

To read more about Gateway's vision, click [here](/).

Gateway offers seamless products for interacting with the protocol. For users, Gateway has custodial and non-custodial solutions for taking ownership of your data.
